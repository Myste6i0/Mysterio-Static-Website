About me

Greetings! I'm Ajith T.G, currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications. Driven by a fervent commitment to self-improvement, I've diligently pursued courses in Programming in Javascript and Introduction to Ethical Hacking, augmenting my skill set. These endeavors have proven instrumental in successfully executing my final year projects.

During my second year, I had the honor of serving as a resource person at Kristhu Jyothi College of Management and Technology, Changanaserry, where I led a seminar on "Cyber Security Awareness," compelling both students and faculty to confront the realities of our digital vulnerabilities.

Fuelled by a passion for technology, I've engaged in immersive workshops on "Internet of Things and Application," culminating in the development of an innovative IoT project titled "Automated Indoor Plant Grow Pot" during my second-year hiatus.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I've undertaken significant projects, including the creation of an Online Food Hub in my fifth semester. This PHP web application allows users to order meals from a single restaurant, streamlining the food ordering process.

Moreover, for my main project in the sixth semester, I developed a machine learning model for hotel booking cancellation prediction. This project utilized advanced algorithms to forecast whether a hotel booking would be canceled or not, aiding in efficient resource allocation and customer satisfaction.

My aspiration is to harness my talents to effect positive change, fostering innovation and societal betterment. Let us all reflect on the opportunities we possess to contribute meaningfully to the world around us.

Projects

IoT

The Automated Plant Cultivation System is an IoT prototype designed to optimize plant care by automatically maintaining soil moisture and light intensity. Using an Arduino module, it ensures plants receive the right amount of water and light. Real-time data is transmitted via an ESP32 WiFi module and can be monitored through a PHP website, allowing users to track conditions remotely without controlling the system.

PHP Web App

Online FoodHub is a robust PHP web application designed to streamline the food ordering process. Users can browse a comprehensive menu from a single restaurant, select their desired food items, and place orders effortlessly. The platform ensures that each order is processed efficiently and delivered promptly to the user's specified location, providing a convenient and reliable dining experience from the comfort of their home.

Machine learning

The Hotel Booking Cancellation Prediction project uses data science and machine learning to forecast cancellations and improve revenue management. Analyzing key features from a detailed dataset, the project trains various models, with the Random Forest Classifier being highly accurate. It includes a Flask-based web interface, offering stakeholders actionable insights and an easy tool for managing cancellations.
